What is a ZirGroup?

ZirGroups are in-app groups that involve features such as:

  • Notes for yourself/group
  • To-Do lists
  • Group Chats
  • Music to hear while working
  • 10 GB group-shared Storage to save files you need
  • A levelling system mostly based on To-Do list
    motivating people to work harder

Frequently Asked Questions

What is ZirApp?

ZirApp is a gamification platform designed to enhance teamwork by turning collaboration into a game-like experience where users can participate, excel, and feel valued.

How does ZirApp help in improving productivity?

ZirApp motivates users through a leveling system based on tasks and to-do lists, encouraging consistent effort and participation in a fun, engaging manner.

What are the different types of accounts available?

ZirApp offers a Basic Account and a Leader Account, each providing different features and levels of access to group creation, joining groups, and personal storage.

Can I get a refund?

ZirApp offers 100% refunds within the first hour of purchase, 80% refund within the first 12 hours of purchase and a 50% refund within the first week of purchase. After a week of purchase refunds will be available only at 10% of the value spent on the account.
